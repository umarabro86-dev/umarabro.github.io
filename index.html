Parrots Diet & Information

Macaw

Diet: Seeds, nuts, fruits, vegetables, and small insects.

Info: Macaws are large, colorful parrots native to Central and South America. They are intelligent and social birds.

Budgerigar (Budgie)

Diet: Seeds, fresh fruits, vegetables, and pellets.

Info: Budgies are small, social, and active parrots, popular as pets around the world.

African Grey Parrot

Diet: Nuts, seeds, fruits, vegetables, and pellets.

Info: African Grey Parrots are known for their intelligence and amazing talking ability.

Lovebird

Diet: Seeds, fruits, vegetables, and pellets.

Info: Lovebirds are small, affectionate parrots that love social interaction and companionship.
